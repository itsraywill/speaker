Step-by-Step Cleaning Process

Finally, after all the above cautions, basic steps, and everything else, I list the step-by-step tutorial for iPhone speaker cleaner. Follow my every step below, and I will explain how to clean speaker dust in 2024 at home. Check this out here:

  1. Turn Off Your iPhone: In the first step, for safety reasons, turn off your iPhone before cleaning to avoid accidental inputs or damage.
  2. Use a Soft Brush or Toothbrush: Slowly brush the speaker grill with a soft bristles brush or toothbrush. This helps to loosen and remove any dust or dirt from your iPhone speakers. Make sure to use a brush with soft bristles to avoid damaging the components.
  3. Use Compressed Air (Optional): If you have compressed air, blow out any remaining dust from the speaker grill. Hold the can upright and keep it a few inches from the grill to avoid moisture inside the phone.
  • Wipe with a Microfiber Cloth: Use a microfiber cloth to wipe the area around the speaker grill.

  • Use a Toothpick or SIM Card Ejector Tool: Gently scrape the dirt from the speaker holes using a toothpick or SIM card ejector tool. Be careful not to insert the tool too deeply or apply too much pressure, as this can damage the speaker.

  • Isopropyl Alcohol (Optional): If oily residues or sticky substances are present, wet a corner of the microfiber cloth with a small amount of isopropyl alcohol and gently wipe the area. Avoid using too much liquid to prevent it from seeping into the phone.

Final Check

Once you have finished cleaning, inspect the speaker grill to ensure that all dirt and debris have been removed.
